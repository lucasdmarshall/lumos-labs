About Mimo's Handicrafts

Preserving traditional craftsmanship with modern elegance

Our Story

Founded with a passion for preserving Myanmar's rich cultural heritage, Mimo's Handicrafts began as a small family workshop in the heart of Yangon. Our journey started with a simple belief: that traditional craftsmanship deserves a place in the modern world.

Today, we work with skilled artisans from across Myanmar, each bringing their unique expertise and cultural knowledge to create beautiful, authentic handicrafts. Every piece tells a story of tradition, skill, and dedication.
